## Delivery notes (for the reviewer, not the engineer)

- Lead with care and candour; open with the standard you're holding so it doesn't read as personal.
- Empathy, not sympathy — connect with the difficulty without lowering the bar.
- Rumble: invite their view on each growth area before concluding; this is a conversation, not a verdict.
- End on the genuine belief that they can close the gap, with the plan as the proof you mean it.
